Most of my professional life has been as a system admin (sysadmin) but at one time I was an expert RPG coder, maintaining custom modifications to ERP software on the IBM AS/400 platform (now known as the IBM eServer iSeries). Recently my experience has been mainly Powershell, C#, and SQL, specifically T-SQL which is the variant used in Microsoft's SQL Server and Sybase's SQLAnywhere.
